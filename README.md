# Dr Stefano Bruno

I am Postdoctoral Research Associate in the School of Mathematics at the University of Edinburgh. I am part of the Probability and Stochastic Analysis group and my current advisor is Prof.  <a href="https://www.turing.ac.uk/people/researchers/sotirios-sabanis">Sotirios Sabanis</a>. I obtained my PhD from the University of Bath in March 2022, under the supervision of Prof. <a href="https://www.uni-muenster.de/AMM/weber/index.shtml">Hendrik Weber</a>. I was part of the <a href="https://samba.ac.uk/"> EPSRC Centre for Doctoral Training in Statistical Applied Mathematics (SAMBa)</a>.
