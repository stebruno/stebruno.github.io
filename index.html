Dr Stefano Bruno

I am Postdoctoral Research Associate in the School of Mathematics at the University of Edinburgh. I am part of the Probability and Stochastic Analysis group and my current advisor is Prof. Sotirios Sabanis. I obtained my PhD from the University of Bath in March 2022, under the supervision of Prof. Hendrik Weber. I was part of the EPSRC Centre for Doctoral Training in Statistical Applied Mathematics (SAMBa).
